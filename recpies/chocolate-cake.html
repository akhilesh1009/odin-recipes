Chocolate Cake

Ingredients:

  • 1 and 1/2 cups flour
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 1/4 cup cocoa powder
  • 1 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1 cup water
  • 1/3 cup vegetable oil
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract

Instructions:

  1. Preheat oven to 180°C (350°F).
  2. Mix dry ingredients in one bowl and wet ingredients in another.
  3. Combine both mixtures and pour into a greased baking pan.
  4. Bake for 30–35 minutes or until a toothpick comes out clean.
  5. Cool and frost as desired before serving.
