Spaghetti

Ingredients:

  • 200g spaghetti
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 2 garlic cloves, minced
  • 400g canned tomatoes
  • Salt and pepper, to taste
  • Grated Parmesan cheese

Instructions:

  1. Cook spaghetti in boiling salted water until al dente, then drain.
  2. Heat olive oil in a pan, add garlic, and sautÃ© for 1 minute.
  3. Add canned tomatoes, salt, and pepper. Simmer for 10 minutes.
  4. Toss the spaghetti in the sauce and serve with Parmesan cheese.
