Caesar Salad

Caesar salad is a crisp and refreshing dish, featuring Romaine lettuce tossed with crunchy croutons, Parmesan cheese, and a tangy Caesar dressing. The dressing, traditionally made with garlic, anchovies, lemon juice, and olive oil, gives the salad its signature bold flavor.

Ingredients:

  • 1 head Romaine lettuce
  • 1/2 cup croutons
  • 1/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 2 tablespoons Caesar dressing
  • Salt and pepper, to taste

Instructions:

  1. Wash and chop the lettuce into bite-sized pieces.
  2. In a large bowl, toss lettuce with croutons and Parmesan cheese.
  3. Add Caesar dressing and mix until evenly coated.
  4. Season with salt and pepper to taste, then serve immediately.
